Return & Refund Policy

At Vastragrah, we adhere to a rigorous quality control process to ensure that our customers receive only the highest quality products.

The products purchased from our website are eligible for return only if they are damaged or have manufacturing defects. Any defective or damaged items must be reported to Vastragrah within 24 hours of receipt. Communications received after this period will not qualify for a return, unless specifically covered by the product warranty, even if the product was delivered incorrectly. Vastragrah reserves the right to determine whether a refund will be issued or not.

We also maintain the right to modify, revoke, or terminate any discount codes at our discretion and without prior notice. This includes the ability to amend this policy at any time. Customers are encouraged to review this policy periodically for any updates or changes.

We recommend inspecting your order upon receipt. If you find that the item is defective, damaged, or if you received the wrong item, please contact us immediately so we can assess the situation and rectify it.

Your purchase is eligible for an exchange only under the following conditions:

  • An incorrect size or product has been delivered (i.e., the product does not match the ordered size).
  • The product you received has a genuine manufacturing defect. If you believe the product is damaged or defective, please take a photograph as soon as you open the packaging.

If your purchase meets the return criteria mentioned above, please contact our customer care team at support@vastragrah.co within 24 hours of delivery with the following information:

  • Order number
  • Delivery address
  • Reason for return (including an image of the item if it is defective or incorrect).

You can contact us via email at support@vastragrah.co from Monday to Saturday, between 10:00 AM and 6:00 PM (IST). We will investigate the issue and respond within 24 working hours.

Please ensure that the product you are returning is unused, unworn, and has its original tags intact. If the product you ordered is eligible for exchange but is unavailable, we will issue a refund instead.
